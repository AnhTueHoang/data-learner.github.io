About Me

Hi! My name is Jenny Hoang. I graduated from DePaul University with a Bachelor of Science in Information Systems, and I've always had a natural curiosity for how things work, why things happen, and what data can reveal behind everyday decisions.

I'm currently working as a Project Coordinator at Amsive. Outside of work, I spend my free time learning tools, practicing analysis techniques, and building data projects—just like the ones featured on this site. My goal is to transition into the data field because I genuinely enjoy exploring datasets, solving problems, and uncovering meaningful stories hidden in numbers.

I hope to pursue a master’s degree in a data-related field in the future. Career-wise, I’m working step by step toward creating a meaningful path in analytics and technology. Personally, I dream of buying a big home one day, traveling the world with my family, and living a life full of curiosity and adventure.

Outside of data, I love traveling, discovering new places, and yes—doing some shopping like many girls do. Life should be both productive and fun.

Thank you so much for stopping by and viewing my work. I hope you enjoy the stories behind the datasets I explore, and I hope my journey inspires you in your own path too.
